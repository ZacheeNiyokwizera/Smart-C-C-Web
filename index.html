About Us

SmartCare Cleaning offers a range of different services that are affordable and responsive to your needs. We understand “one-size-fits-all” solutions rarely meet every job specification and therefore are committed to designing a solution specifically catered to your space, budget, and timing. Each client is unique and in today’s pandemic times, most businesses no longer operate from 9 to 5. If you are in search of a professional and flexible cleaning company in Cape Town, search no longer.

SmartCare Cleaning was established in 2011, but many of the staff have been in the industry for over ten years, and take joy and pride in their work. More than anything, our people are our key asset and they are passionate about what they do, knowing the value of cleanliness and hygiene. Our teams strive to achieve the highest possible levels of service excellence and customer satisfaction.

We also understand the deeply personal nature of cleaning services, and ensure to deliver a discreet, respectful and safe service. New staff are taken through a stringent hiring process, including all the necessary background and reference checks plus a training period. This ensures that we protect not only the physical assets of our clients, but also their privacy and reputation.

We prioritise long-term relationships with our clients, no matter what the size. We have an enduring commitment to getting the job done, and will prioritise results over hourly inputs.

Clean home. Clean Mind. We Care.

OUR CLEANING

SERVICES

DEEP CLEANING FOR RESIDENTIAL AND COMMERCIAL PROPERTY

When there is tough dirt and grime that has to be cleaned out of your home or office you require deep cleaning services. A deep clean is suitable for a very dirty home that has not been cleaned in a while. You may be moving into a home where the previous occupants kept the home in a very dirty state, a deep clean is suitable in such situations. If there are grease marks on walls, spilt oils, dirt and grime behind your stove, tough stains in shower tiles and glasses, then you need a deep clean. It includes cleaning of all windows, cupboards and shelves, cleaning of fridges and ovens, cleaning of mould off walls.Our deep cleaning is a once off clean that is very thorough and we ensure to clean all those hard to reach areas of your home or office.

AFTER BUILDERS CLEANING (POST-CONSTRUCTION)

When the builders have finished renovating and refurbishing your property the last thing that you will want in your new home is the smell of paint, inhaling dust and seeing grime or stickers on the wall. We offer post construction cleaning where we assist in clearing any mess that builders would have left behind after doing house or building renovations.We remove all excess tile grout and paint marks which the builders would have left behind. Post-renovation cleaning is a great way to impose closure on your building project.

CARPET CLEANING

When you have a tough and stubborn stain on your carpet, then our carpet cleaning offering is for you. Our methods remove all dirt embedded deep down in the carpets and we leave your carpets fresh and dry in no time. We use a deep carpet penetrating steam cleaning method which ensures hot water penetrates deep into the carpet fibres and the high pressure suction pump sucks up the moisture which lifts up the dirt and dust mites from deep inside the carpet fibres. The cleaning suction action cleaning is reinforced by our germ killing and environmentally friendly chemicals which are dissolved in the hot water used for cleaning. We are also experts at cleaning upholstery ranging from couches, chairs, mattresses, ottomans and upholstery seats.

OFFICE CLEANING

When you want to create an environment of professionalism, cleanliness and order in your work environment, our office cleaning services are for you. Whether you run a small business or are part of a large corporation, ensuring that your offices are kept clean and tidy helps to create positive impression with your clients and also improves employee morale. From ensuring that the coffee cups are washed and dried to cleaning the bathrooms, SmartCare CleaningServices can offer you with office cleaning services to handle all your office cleaning tasks, large and small. You have the option to hire us once-off, or on a more regular cadence.

WINDOW CLEANING

When you cannot see through your dirt-stained windows anymore, our window cleaning services are just for you. The window washing service is done using water fed poles which is a more effective cleaning method compared to the traditional window cleaning methods. Our window washing services are reliable and we guarantee best results at all times. We ensure the windows are left with no streaks or marks.Our window cleaning equipment allows us to reach heights of 25 metres without the use of ladders or any access equipment allowing technicians to stay on the ground therefore complying with all aspects of Health and Safety. This forms part of all of our deep cleaning Services and we also offer this as a separate service, charging for the cleaning of each.

DOMESTIC SERVICES

When you just need some extra help in giving your home a general clean, then our domestic services are for you. These services include tasks such as general surface cleaning, vacuuming, mopping, cleaning of dishes, laundry services. You can either have this service as once-off or on a regular weekly basis. No cleaning products and tools are included, only lunch and transport to the venue. We prioritise long-term and trusting relationships with our clients, and therefore are flexible and adaptive to unique service requests.

HANDYMAN SERVICES

When you have just moved into a new home, and you are struggling to hang your favourite, delicate mirror, our handyman services are just for you. We offer a range of different services from removing nails and filling the holes with polyfilla; installation of shelves; fitting of TV brackets; hanging of pictures and mirrors; fitting of curtain rails; filling of blinds; repairs to kitchen cupboards/ wardrobes, you name it! We use our tools with pride ensuring accuracy, care and perfection.
